How to use

Once installed, WAVE can be launched from your extensions bar. It will appear as a circle with a W in the centre.

When selected, WAVE will open up to the side of your browser window and automatically starts the checks.

You will need to select the details of the checks you want to appear visually on the screen. Within 'view details', select only alerts and errors to be flagged.
