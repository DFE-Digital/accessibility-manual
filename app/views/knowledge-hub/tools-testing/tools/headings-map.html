How to use {{pageName}}

Once installed, HeadingsMap can be accessed from your extensions bar. It appears as a letter h with a forward slash.

Once selected, it will then appear and show the heading hierarchy of your webpage.

Results

View larger image in a new tab

HeadingsMap will show the heading structure of the page, with the hierarchy of headings displayed. This will help you to understand the structure of the page and identify any issues with the heading structure.

What to do next

The team should review the heading structure to make sure they are correct.

It’s best practice to only have one heading level 1 (H1), which should be unique, and be contained in the title for the browser. The structure should then follow a hierarchical structure.
