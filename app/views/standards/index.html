Explore and understand the standards

Guidance for the accessible and inclusive design standards, how you can meet them, and how they are assured.

Standard: DDTS-812

Accessibility statements

Services must publish a statement of accessibility for the websites and mobile applications of which they are the owners. This is required in law under:

  • The Public Sector Bodies (Websites and Mobile Applications) (No. 2) Accessibility Regulations 2018
Standard: DDTS-813

Report and manage accessibility issues

Services must record and manage all accessibility issues in their products and services in the central DfE accessibility issue register.

Standard: DDTS-814

Audit products and websites

Services must have an accessibility audit, before a service is used by end users

How we help teams meet these standards

Not all teams are the same and the DesignOps and Accessibility team are here to help everyone and apply these standards in their role.

  • Standards workshops in team meetings and community
  • Training on the application and compliance of standards
  • Access to the monthly accessibility clinic
