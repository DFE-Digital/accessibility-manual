Our accessibility standards

Accessibility standards services in the Department for Education must meet through the DDT Standards framework.

Published

Publish an accurate accessibility statement

Services must publish a statement of accessibility for the websites and mobile applications of which they are the owners.

Draft

Report and manage accessibility issues

Services must record and manage all accessibility issues in their products and services in the central DfE accessibility issue register.

Draft

Audit products and websites

All products and websites must have an accessibility audit, and be reaudited every 2 years.
