Our standards

Accessibility standards we expect services in the Department for Education to meet through the DDT Standards framework.

Published

Publish an accurate accessibility statement

Services must publish a statement of accessibility for the websites and mobile applications of which they are the owners.

Draft

Report and manage accessibility issues

Services must record and manage all accessibility issues in their products and services in the central DfE accessibility issue register.

Draft

Audit products and websites

All products and websites must have an accessibility audit, and be reaudited every 2 years.

Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)

WCAG is a set of guidelines for making web content more accessible to people with disabilities. It is the international standard for web accessibility and is enforced in UK Law.

Understand the criteria

Understand each of the WCAG criteria, how to apply them and common issues.

Understand conformance

Understand the 3 levels of conformance, A, AA, and AAA and what they mean.
