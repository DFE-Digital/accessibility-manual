DesignOps will ensure compliance with the DfE Accessibility Statement Digital Standard through a combination of the following methods:

Monitoring

Ad-hoc checks

Regular reviews of accessibility statements will be carried to ensure ongoing adherence to the standard.

These checks will be carried out by the DesignOps team.

Any issues identified will be raised with the service owner for resolution.

Service assessments

Evaluation against the standard will be assured in service assessments at beta phase onwards.

This standard will be assured by the assessor panel and failure of the standard will be recorded as a red rating against standard 5 of the Service Standard.

Reporting

Compliance with the standard will be reported on the service health check hub (DfE Intranet).
