How to test for different disabilities and impairments

Understand the impact of different impairments and how to use a range of tools and techniques.

These will help you check your product or website for any barriers or issues.

Vision

Being able to use sight to interact with or understand content can be affected by a range of conditions, including:

  • colour blindness
  • low vision
  • blindness
  • eye injuries
  • bright or low light conditions
  • older devices and low screen resolution

Understand the impact that visual impairments can have on users when using digital products and websites.

Tools and techniques to help you test for visual impairments.

Hearing

Hearing can be affected by a range of conditions, including:

  • hearing loss
  • deafness
  • tinnitus
  • ear infections
  • ear injuries

Understand the impact that hearing issues can have on users when using digital products and websites.
