We are raising the standard of accessibility in our services

1 in 5 people in the UK have some form of disability. We want to create a culture where accessibility isn't an optional step, where it's not an afterthought, and it's not a tick box exercise at the end of a project.

We want to see accessible practices and inclusive design principles, embedded into all phases of the service delivery lifecycle.

Our vision

We believe everyone should be able to use our products and services without facing barriers.

We've also published our roadmap and details of our work so far.
