We're raising the standard of accessibility in our services

1 in 5 people in the UK have some form of disability. We want to create a culture where accessibility isn't an optional step, where it's not an afterthought, or a tick-box exercise at the end of a project.

We want to see accessible practices and inclusive design principles, embedded into all phases of the service delivery lifecycle.

Our 4 priorities

1. Work with people and professions

2. Develop skills, knowledge and awareness

3. Improve accessibility and inclusive design culture across DfE

4. Create a sustainable and scalable approach to compliance and assurance
