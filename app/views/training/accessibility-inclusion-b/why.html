Accessibility and inclusion

Why accessibility is important

There are 3 main reasons to ensure our products and websites are accessible

It's the law

As a public body, we have a legal requirement under the Public Sector Websites and Applications Regulations 2018.

This law requires us to make sure all our websites and applications, meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) Level AA as a minimum.

There are other laws and regulations we must follow and comply with, you can read about these, along with guidance around WCAG and what level AA means, in the knowledge hub.

It's not just a legal requirement, it's also the right thing to do.
