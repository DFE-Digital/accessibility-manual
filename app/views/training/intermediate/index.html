This training is designed to be a follow-up to the introduction to accessibility and inclusive design training.

Learning outcomes

By the end of this self-guided training, you will expand your knowledge on:

  • accessibility audits
  • accessibility testing
  • assistive technology, for example, screen readers and voice control
  • inclusive design
  • WCAG

You can complete the 20 questions in as many sessions as you like. Depending on your experience and knowledge, the training could take up to 45 minutes.

Your progress will be saved each time you answer a question.
