This training is a series of 20 questions designed to be a follow-up to the basic accessibility and inclusive design training.

The questions will check your knowledge of the topics covered in the wider accessibility manual. Each one has a link to the relevant section of the manual to help you if you need it.

You should complete the basic accessibility training first.

Learning outcomes

By the end of this self-guided training, you will expand your knowledge on:

  • accessibility audits
  • accessibility testing
  • assistive technology, for example, screen readers and voice control
  • inclusive design
  • WCAG

Complete the training in as many sessions as you like. Depending on your experience and knowledge, it could take up to 45 minutes.

Your progress will be saved each time you answer a question.
