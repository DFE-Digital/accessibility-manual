Accessibility is about ensuring that everyone can use our digital services to achieve the outcomes they need.

This includes making sure our websites, mobile apps, and digital content are usable by people with a variety of disabilities.

Inclusive design involves creating products and services that are accessible to, and usable by, as many people as possible irrespective of ability.

It anticipates diversity in users and aims to address the needs of all people, including those with disabilities, right from the start of the design process.
