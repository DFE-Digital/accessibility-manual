Accessibility is about ensuring that people with disabilities can use our products, services, and documents without encountering barriers.

This means making sure our websites, mobile apps, and digital content are usable by people with a variety of disabilities.

This applies to everyone we provide services for. For example, members of the public, learners, school professionals, and our own staff.
