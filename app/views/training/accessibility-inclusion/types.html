Anyone can experience different types of disabilities. Through awareness and understanding of these and how they affect people, we can create a more inclusive environment for everyone.

Disabilities can fall into the following areas:

Vision

This includes people who are blind, have low vision, or are colour blind.

Hearing

This includes people who are deaf or hard of hearing.

Motor

This includes people with mobility impairments, missing limbs, or limited dexterity.

Cognitive

This includes people with learning disabilities, memory issues, or cognitive impairments.

We will explore these in more detail and help you understand how you can prevent exclusion of people with each type of disability.
