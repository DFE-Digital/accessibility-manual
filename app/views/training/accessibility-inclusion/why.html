Why accessibility matters

There are a few reasons why accessibility is important:

It's the law

As a public body we are required, by law, to ensure our products and websites meet accessibility guidelines.

The primary law for websites and applications is called the Public Sector Bodies Websites and Applications Regulations 2018.

There are other laws that apply to us around accessibility, and you can read about these in the knowledge hub

There are 86 criteria that are detailed in the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines, often referred to as WCAG
