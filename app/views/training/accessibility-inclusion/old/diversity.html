Put people at the centre of the design process from the start, and throughout. People with disabilities can often provide a unique perspective to a problem, also ensure to include people with a diverse background in this process, including age, cultures, ability, and background.

For example

Running co-design sessions with people with diverse perspectives will lead to more innovative solutions that might not be apparent with a less diverse team.

Ensure that this diversity continues through testing and involve users in the design and feedback process. This helps to maintain a product or website that works for more people and is less likely to exclude people.
