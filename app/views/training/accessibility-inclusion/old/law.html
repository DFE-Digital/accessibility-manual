The main law that we must comply with is the Public Sector Bodies Websites and Applications Accessibility Regulations (sometimes referred to as PSBAR). This came into effect in September 2019.

The law requires:

All content provided through a browser must meet WCAG 2.2 to Level AA.

There are other laws that are also applicable, including:

  • Equality Act 2010
  • Public Sector Equality Duty
  • EN 301 549 Accessibility Requirements for ICT Products and Services

You can find out more about these laws and regulations in the knowledge hub.
