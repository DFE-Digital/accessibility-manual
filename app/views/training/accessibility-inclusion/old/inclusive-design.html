Inclusive design is an approach that aims to create products and services that are usable by as many people as possible, regardless of their diverse abilities, backgrounds, and circumstances.

With a focus on recognising and addressing potential exclusions, learning from the diverse needs and perspectives of users, and developing solutions that benefit a wide range of individuals.

Inclusive design is covered by 3 areas:

  • Recognise exclusion
  • Learn from diversity
  • Solve for one, extend to many
