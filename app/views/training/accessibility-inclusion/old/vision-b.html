When publishing content which uses a colour-based status indicator, ensure there is text to help users to understand the status.

This doesn’t just help people who struggle with seeing certain colours, it helps everyone understand the content more easily.

Do

Use text alongside colour to help people understand the context.

Don't

Don't use colour alone as people with colour deficiencies can see different colours to what you might intend.
