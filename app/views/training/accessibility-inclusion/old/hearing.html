People who are deaf or hard of hearing need alternative ways to access audio information. This includes audio-only content and video with audio.

1 in 3 adults in the UK are deaf, have hearing loss or tinnitus.

Providing captions in video where there is spoken audio, ensures that people who are deaf can still understand spoken content.

This doesn't just support people who are deaf, this also helps people who may be in a noisy environment and cannot hear the audio well, or where the spoken language is different to theirs and find it difficult to understand.
