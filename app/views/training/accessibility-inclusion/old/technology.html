Assistive technology (AT) is software utilised by people with disabilities and neurodiverse people to assist them in navigating and interacting with websites, apps and digital content.

This technology provides a way for people to use their hardware and software in a way that may be difficult, or impossible without AT.

There are many different types of assistive technology, these include screen readers, voice control software, magnification tools as well as literacy tools.
