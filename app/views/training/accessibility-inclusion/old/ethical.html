Why accessibility is important

There are 3 main reasons to ensure our products and websites are accessible

It's ethical

Accessibility is a about fairness and equity. Providing accessible access to services, products, and information demonstrates a commitment to inclusivity and respect for all individuals, regardless of their abilities or capabilities.

We should ensure that when we're designing products and services, we include people in the design stage with a diverse background to inform that process.

We should also test with as many people as possible from a range of backgrounds, technical ability and skills, and with people with and without disabilities or who use assistive or adaptive technologies. You can learn more about what these and a how to do this in the knowledge hub.

Ensuring products and services are designed this way, means they work for everyone, not just those with impairments.
