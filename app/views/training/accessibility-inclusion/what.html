Accessibility is about ensuring that everyone can use our digital services to achieve the outcomes they need.

This includes making sure our websites, mobile apps, and digital content are usable by people with a variety of disabilities.

We must prioritise the people we are designing for, placing them at the centre of what it is we are designing.

This enables us to understand people's needs and how they might use our products and websites, enabling us to provide the best possible experience.
