People with mobility impairments or limited dexterity, including those with conditions like cerebral palsy or arthritis, or those who have lost limbs, may find it challenging, or impossible, to use a mouse or other pointing devices.

Around 1 in 5 people have a mobility or dexterity impairment in the UK.

We must ensure that all interactive elements such as forms and links are accessible via keyboard navigation.

It's also important that these interactive elements, can be controlled using voice commands.

You don't need special software to test these features. You can find guidance in the tools and testing section of this manual for how to use tools to test these features.
