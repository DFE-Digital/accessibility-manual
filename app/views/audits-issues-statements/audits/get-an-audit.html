You should start planning for your audit at alpha phase, and ensure that sufficient time, resource and cost have been planned into your delivery.

This will allow you to have an audit, update your accessibility statement and resolve any issues before users start to use your product or website.

Get help to plan and organise your audit

Internal-facing products

If your product or website is internal-facing, for example WorkDay or a DfE SharePoint site, email the Business Engagement and Accessibility Team.

External-facing products

Submit an audit request to DesignOps for all other types of DfE products or websites.

They will help you to:

  • understand what type of audit you need
  • plan your audit timeline
  • prepare the right information for auditors
  • find the right audit provider

DesignOps will aim to respond to requests within 5 working days.
