Designers often perform a mix of design, research, and testing activities. Even if you're mainly an interaction designer, make sure you review the guidance for other roles that relevant to your work.

What interaction designers do

Interaction designers focus on creating engaging interfaces with well thought out behaviours. They consider the user's journey through the service, and make sure the interface is easy to use.

The core skills of interaction designers, require them to understand:

  • animation
  • colour theory
  • graphic design elements
  • HTML and CSS
  • navigation techniques and practices
  • prototyping, wireframing, and mockups
  • typography
  • user research and testing
  • web analytics

In addition to the above skills, interaction designers also benefit from an understanding of the laws of UX.
