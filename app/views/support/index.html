Support with digital delivery and user-centred design

The DesignOps team can help with questions, advice, and support for the following topics:

  • accessibility statements
  • audits
  • content design
  • design UI components and patterns
  • standards compliance or assurance
  • technical guidance with code and development

Contact the DesignOps team

  • Email: design.ops@education.gov.uk
  • DfE Slack: #designops
  • UK Governemnt Slack: #dfe-designops

Support with devices, workplace adjustments and assisitve technology

The accessibility team, can help with support and advice on work-related questions, including:

  • assistive software
  • creating accessible documents and presentations
  • using Microsoft products
  • workplace adjustments

Visit the Accessibility hub on the DfE Intranet for more information, or to contact the accessibility team.
