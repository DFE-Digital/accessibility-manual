Support for digital delivery and user-centred design

DesignOps can help with questions, advice, and support for:

  • accessibility statements
  • audits
  • content design
  • design UI components and patterns
  • standards compliance or assurance
  • technical guidance with code and development

Request support from DesignOps

Complete the online form to request support with an accessibility query.

Other ways to contact DesignOps

  • Email: design.ops@education.gov.uk
  • DfE Slack: #designops
  • UK Government Slack: #dfe-designops

Support with devices, workplace adjustments and assisitive technology

The accessibility team, can help with support and advice on work-related questions, including:

  • assistive software
  • creating accessible documents and presentations
  • using Microsoft products
  • workplace adjustments

Request support from the accessibility team

Visit the digital accessibility hub on the DfE intranet for more information, or email the accessibility team.
