Get help in the workplace

The DfE Accessibility team, can help with support and advice on work-related accessibility questions, including:

  • Workplace adjustments
  • Assistive technology
  • Creating accessible content for a range of channels
  • Running training sessions with you and your team

The team also run and manage booking for the Experience Lab (opens in new tab) in the Sheffield office.
